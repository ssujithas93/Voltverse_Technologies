Exhibited @ IMTEX FORMING 2026

IMTEX FORMING 2026 is one of Asia’s leading international exhibitions focused on forming and manufacturing technologies. The exhibition is organised by the Indian Machine Tool Manufacturers’ Association (IMTMA) and brings together machine tool manufacturers, automation solution providers, technology innovators, and academic institutions from across the globe. Voltverse_Technologies participated in this prestigious event under the i2 Academia Pavilion, providing students an opportunity to showcase industry-oriented innovation projects and gain direct exposure to real-world manufacturing environments.
